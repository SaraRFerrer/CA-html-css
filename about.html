About Us

Welcome to Conscious Coffee, we are a Norwegian based coffee shop with nine different locations around Norway. Conscious Coffee was founded in 2010 by an Italian family who moved to Norway in 2005.

Our Coffee is made with the finest coffee beans harvested on the South American part of the Bean Belt and are migrated directly to Conscious Coffee and distributed to all our Café’s. This way we ensure the best quality to all our costumers.
